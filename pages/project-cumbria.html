KARL BAYLY
Home in Cumbria

FUZZYVIBES, Auckland NZ

September 2015
-

“Home in Cumbria” presents a series of work gesturing at antiquated ideals pulled from late 19th and the Mid-20th century. Exploring varied perspectives that look at a language of consumption and idealism.

Influenced by Logan's Run (1976), where Jessica 6 and Logan 5 (main protagonist's) escape from the dome city and are confronted with a perplexing and disturbing dystopian site. In the space of 12 seconds their expressions flicker between urgency, confusion and intrigue as they witness a giant glowing ball rising in the distance.

“What is it?” as Jessica 6 asks “I don't know. Whatever it is, it's warm.
