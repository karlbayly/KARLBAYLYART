Engaging the figure of the tourist or travel writer, Loverdose Tattoo brings together five artists from outside of Hamilton. Lauren Burrow, Eleanor Cooper, Melanie Kung Vanessa Preston and Evangeline Riddiford Graham explore interactions between places, distances, words, bodies and objects. From perusing the perfume sections of New York department stores, to a brief meeting on the main street in Hamilton on a Friday night, to stolen moments in transit or on hold for a call centre, Loverdose Tattoo responds to multiple locales and situations, embracing the nature of dialogue at a distance.

This exhibition is the first half of an exchange between Pilot and North Projects.

An accompanying publication designed by Gemma Banks and Alice Bush will be available at the exhibition opening for a small Koha.
